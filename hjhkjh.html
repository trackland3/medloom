Gallbladder Removal Surgery

A 45-year-old patient presented with severe upper right abdominal pain, nausea, and jaundice. Following a comprehensive evaluation and diagnostic imaging, including an ultrasound and MRI, the patient was diagnosed with cholecystitis and gallstones. An urgent laparoscopic cholecystectomy was performed by an expert surgical team via e-channeling services. The procedure was completed successfully, and the patient had a rapid recovery with minimal post-operative pain, showcasing the convenience and effectiveness of remote surgical consultations and scheduling.

Gastric Sleeve Surgery

A 35-year-old patient seeking weight loss solutions presented with a high BMI and related health issues such as hypertension and sleep apnea. After a comprehensive evaluation and diagnostic tests, including an upper GI endoscopy and abdominal ultrasound, the patient was deemed a suitable candidate for bariatric surgery. A laparoscopic gastric sleeve procedure was performed by a specialized surgical team via e-channeling services. The surgery went well, and the patient experienced a rapid recovery with minimal discomfort, illustrating the convenience effectiveness of remote surgical consultations and scheduling.
