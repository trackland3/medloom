Gastric Sleeve Surgery

A 35-year-old patient seeking weight loss solutions presented with a high BMI and
related health issues such as hypertension and sleep apnea. After a comprehensive
evaluation and diagnostic tests, including an upper GI endoscopy and abdominal
ultrasound, the patient was deemed a suitable candidate for bariatric surgery.
A laparoscopic gastric sleeve procedure was performed by a specialized surgical
team via e-channeling services. The surgery went well, and the patient experienced
a rapid recovery with minimal discomfort, illustrating the convenience and
