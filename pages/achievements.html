Laparoscopic Appendectomy

Recently, a 32-year-old patient presented with acute abdominal pain
and tenderness in the lower right quadrant. After thorough evaluation
and diagnostic imaging,including ultrasound and CT scans,the patient
was diagnosed with acute appendicitis. Urgent laparoscopic appendectomy
was performed by a skilled surgical team via e-channeling services.The
procedure went smoothly, and the patient experienced a quick recovery
with minimal post-operative discomfort, highlighting the efficiency and
effectiveness of remote surgical consultations and bookings.
